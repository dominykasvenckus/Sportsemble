# Sportsemble
Sportsemble is a mobile app made with React Native, Expo, and Firebase, designed to help people discover and organize sports activities in their local area. Whether you're looking to join a game or create your own, this app connects you with others who share your passion for sports. It also features a chat function to help users communicate easily, as well as the ability to follow others and see which activities they participate in.

## Features
- **Discover activities**: Find sports activities happening nearby.
- **Filter activities**: Filter events by sport type and proximity to your location.
- **Create events**: Organize your own sports activities.
- **Chat with others**: Connect with other users through an integrated chat feature to coordinate and discuss activities.
- **Follow users**: Follow other users and see the activities they participate in.
